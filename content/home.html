The Brew Gentlemen Beer Company is a microbrewery located in the historic steel town of Braddock, PA. For the latest details on Brew Gentlemen Events, you can visit our blog. If you prefer to stay updated via social media, please follow us on twitter, like us on facebook, and observe our artistry on instagram. We would like to encourage you to connect with us on social media and join the growing craft beer community.

We hope that you will visit our tap room and experience our take on modern craft beer. If you are interested in learning more, you can also tour the brewery while you enjoy a delicious beverage on tap.

Most importantly, join our mailing list to stay updated! Don't worry, we won't spam you and you will get first registration opportunities when we do something special.
