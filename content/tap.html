EAST COAST STYLE INDIA PALE ALE • 7.4% ABV • MALT: PALE, MUNICH, CRYSTAL 80 • HOPS: NUGGET, CENTENNIAL
A revival of an old brand name from Braddock’s past, General Braddock’s IPA is a bold and flavorful East Coast India Pale Ale. It balances smooth caramel sweetness with a crisp hop finish.
PAIRING NOTES: This bittersweet, malt-centric IPA is best with rich, savory, and spicy foods. It cuts through the thickness of juicy burgers and roasted meats and emphasizes the heat of Mexican food, jerk chicken, and sausage.
NON-TRADITIONAL WHEAT BEER • 5.6% ABV • MALT: PALE, RED WHEAT • HOPS: NORTHERN BREWER, LIBERTY, MT. HOOD • ADDITIONAL: CARDAMOM, CINNAMON, CLOVES, GINGER, BLACK PEPPER
The sky was bright white on the chilly fall day that we first brewed our chai-spiced wheat beer. We’ve come a long way since then, but the gigantic clouds of white steam that billow from the towering blue steel mill at the end of town serve as a reminder of where it all began.
PAIRING NOTES: The subtle warmth of cinnamon, ginger, cardamom, cloves and black pepper adds complexity to lighter seafood dishes such as sushi, tempura, and shellfish. Add garlic, lemon, shallots and butter for a great mussel broth.
SESSION RED ALE • 4.20% ABV • MALT: PALE, MUNICH, CRYSTAL 60, CRYSTAL 120, CHOCOLATE • HOPS: EAST KENT GOLDINGS
A balanced and easy-drinking session ale, Business Casual is that which we ourselves required: a refreshing and flavorful drink-while-working beer. its smooth caramel malt sweetness gives way to a lightly roasted and dry finish.
PAIRING NOTES: This malt-forward red ale is an excellent companion to roasted and barbecued meats. Its unassertive simplicity makes it an obvious choice to pair with more basic dishes, such as beef stew, shepherd’s pie, or grilled chicken.
WHEAT STOUT • 8.2% ABV • MALT: PALE, WHITE WHEAT, CRYSTAL 120, MIDNIGHT WHEAT, ROASTED BARLEY, ROASTED WHEAT • HOPS: NORTHERN BREWER
From the renovation of an old electrical supply store to the revitalization of an entire region, the renewal process is full of both creation and destruction. Build and Destroy, our ultra-smooth wheat stout, is a tribute to forward progress.
PAIRING NOTES: The rich, roasted flavors of this stout are best paired with similarly rich, roasted foods. Black malts stand up to the char of a filet mignon, and the creamy texture adds depth to thick chocolate desserts.
